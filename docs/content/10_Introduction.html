The MathLingua Language

MathLingua is a language for precisely encoding mathematical knowledge to form smart encyclopedias of mathematics.

Today there is so much mathematical knowledge one cannot keep it all in their head. Further, traditional digital encyclopedias of mathematics fall short when it is hard to explore and understand their content.

What we need is a language that can conveniently encode mathematics such that a computer can also easily understand it to help you explore the knowledge.

MathLingua is that language.
